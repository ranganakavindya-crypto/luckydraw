Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the lottery? +

Select any available number between 1-100 on the Purchase page, complete the payment of LKR 100, and your ticket will be confirmed instantly. The winning number is drawn daily at midnight.

How is the winning number selected? +

The winning number is selected using a certified random number generator at midnight daily. The process is audited and verified to ensure complete fairness and transparency.

How do I receive my prize if I win? +

Winners are contacted directly via SMS and email within 24 hours of the draw. To claim your LKR 10,000 prize, you'll need to verify your identity through our secure portal. Prizes are transferred directly to your bank account within 3 business days.

Can I book multiple numbers? +

Yes! You can book as many available numbers as you like. Each number costs LKR 100. Many players increase their chances by booking multiple numbers each day.

What happens if no one books the winning number? +

If the winning number hasn't been booked by any player, the prize money rolls over to the next day, creating an even bigger jackpot. This is clearly stated in our Terms & Conditions.
